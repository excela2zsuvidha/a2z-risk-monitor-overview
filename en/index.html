2 What we watch for — 8 fraud signals

Each rule encodes a fraud pattern A2Z has historically seen. Thresholds tunable in real-time without redeploys.
🆕 New Merchant Going Big
Brand-new account suddenly pushing ₹1L+
📈 5× Growth (new)
New merchant doing 5× their first-week volume
📈 10× Growth (existing)
Established merchant doing 10× their normal day
⚡ Velocity Burst
Huge transaction spike inside one hour
🌙 Off-Hours Activity
Active 2-5 AM when normally inactive
↩️ Refund Storm
Refund rate suddenly 3× the baseline
🔄 Service Drift
Switches to a service type they never used
🎯 Single Beneficiary
80%+ of payouts to one bank account

5 How it gets better over time

Three layers of continuous improvement. Ops feedback drives the most important one.
📊 Auto-adapting baselines
Every night, baselines rebuild from last 7 days. Merchants who legitimately grow won't keep firing alerts — only sudden jumps trigger.
🤖 AI judgment refines
Claude reads richer merchant context as we add data. We tune the prompt — no model retraining needed, no compliance issue.
👥 Ops feedback (most important)
Every "Acknowledge" / "Resolve" action is recorded. Weekly review: rules misfiring >30% get loosened. Missed cases get new rules. Hot-reload, no deploy.
